About Me

I’m a Senior Data Scientist with over 3 years of experience building and scaling machine learning, optimization, and cloud-based AI solutions. I hold a Master’s degree in Data Science from Indiana University Bloomington (GPA: 4.0/4.0), and my expertise spans machine learning, demand forecasting, optimization algorithms, A/B testing, data engineering, and full-stack model deployment.

My core strength lies in developing scalable ML pipelines and applying advanced statistical and optimization techniques to solve large-scale infrastructure and business challenges. Recently at Toyota, I led a $150M optimization initiative by designing predictive scheduling and production planning models using constrained linear programming (GurobiPy), significantly enhancing supply chain efficiency and manufacturing throughput.

Expanding into Generative AI, I developed and deployed an internal Q&A system ("AskToyota") leveraging LLaMA2, FAISS, and LangChain to streamline access to critical forecasting and scheduling documentation. This tool improved operational decision-making across business units and demonstrated my ability to deploy secure, production-ready LLM solutions in an enterprise environment.

I have extensive hands-on experience with cloud platforms like AWS and Azure, where I designed solutions for real-time data processing, scalable model training (SageMaker), automated ML pipelines (Airflow), and containerized API deployments (Docker, FastAPI). My background includes building robust ETL pipelines, managing big data processing with PySpark and Redshift, and ensuring high-quality data flows into production ML systems.

With deep experience in supply chain analytics, demand modeling, optimization, and Generative AI, I am passionate about applying cutting-edge machine learning and cloud technologies to drive real business impact. I bring a strong focus on operational scalability, cost savings, and innovation to every project.
